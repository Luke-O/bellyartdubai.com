Meet our teachers:

“Great dancers are not great because of their technique, they are great because of their passion"- Martha Graham

Belly Art School is based in Argentina. Since 2013 we have started offering our classes in many different dance schools in Dubai. We believe that Oriental Dance it is an art form and gives each student dancer the chance to express themselves in many different ways.

At Belly Art Dubai Dance School we are dedicated to provide the higher oriental dance education in an atmosphere of love, fun and respect. Our focus consists in creating professional artists, for those who are willing to accept the challenge.
