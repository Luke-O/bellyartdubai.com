About Belly Art

Belly Art School was founded in 2008 in Argentina by Marilyn Barrios where it is still running. It has been recently established in Dubai as Belly Art FZE and already counts a variety of major Arabic dance shows. We are proud to say that Belly Art FZE are the ONLY school in the Middle-East that provides serious oriental dance education in line with strong values of professionalism, respect of cultural heritage and creativity. We provide entertainment shows with quality, professionalism & variety.​ “Belly Art” provides first class entertainment for wedding​s, life events, corporate events & rest​au​rant entertainment.

Education

At Belly Art School we are dedicated to provide the higher oriental dance education in an atmosphere of love, fun and respect. Our focus consists in creating professional artists, for those who are willing to accept the challenge

Our Values

Trust & respect: we are transparent & respectful with our clients and our audience. Passion: we are passionate artists, we love our job! Simplicity: we are easy going! We love dancing, laughing, and being positive! Integrity: we walk our talk and keep our word. Quality: we deliver excellency in everything we do.

Belly Art School has four characterizes core values: Passion, Commitment, respect and love for art, for people who like both attending School. We know that Belly Dance is an art who can allow express enable personal expression in many different ways. Belly Art aims to provide students with an advanced education while delivering the best possible preparation for optimal scenic development, and to train professionals to be aware of the proper conduct of belly dance to enrich the culture by providing the most refined art in oriental dance.
Here in Belly Art our goal is to educate the public about belly dance as an art and as well to give dancers the tools and skills to showcase themselves top quality. This does not only show the technical of Belly Art, but also proven methodology requirements for developing the career of our students and professional dancers. Company plan for the future includes an International Course belly art style focused only for professional dancers, who introduce them into the culture and open a new opportunities in the market of the belly dance world to promote Arabic Dance as an Art and express it through a strong sense of creativity.

Our main opportunities of artistic dance education program are: Belly Dance Instructor, Professional Belly dance , Beginner level, Intermediate, Advanced level, Professional level, Workshops & Master Class will be taught by renowned teachers locally and around the world.

• Belly Dance Instructor:

Students will follow up our study program to become an instructor, we start teaching the basic steps to advanced steps. (Certification & study book provided)

• Professional Belly dancer:

Students will learn the secrets be learning the basics in order to become a professional dancer, this course includes ballet, theatre, contemporary. (Certification & study book provided)

Also, we offer monthly a master class & workshops that will be taught by renowned teachers locally and around the world.

Belly Art School in Argentina

Belly Art School was born with a different consciousness about the art of belly dance. We believe that Oriental Dance it is an art form and gives each student dancer the chance to express themselves in many different ways.

Since 2013 we have started offering our classes in Argentina and Dubai. BELLY ART, being the first dance school in Dubai with a formal and professional career path. Our trainings and exams led students to a formal certification of their skills.
